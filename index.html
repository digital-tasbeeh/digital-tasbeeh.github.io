About

Welcome to Digital Tasbeeh Counter, a simple, modern, and easy-to-use tasbeeh counter app designed for your daily dhikr, salawat, dua and meditations. Are you looking for all in one zikr counter app? Need a dedicated durood counter? Or do you want to replace your physical beads with a beautiful digital tasbih app? This app is built to be a helpful companion for your daily dhikr (zikr).

You can easily count your prayers, salawat, and zikr without losing track. This Islamic tasbeeh stays in your pocket, ready whenever you need it.

Why Choose Digital Tasbeeh?
The tasbeeh counter is designed with simplicity and daily routine in mind. The focus is on offering a clean and reliable experience so you can concentrate on your prayers without distractions.

Core Features:

• Simple & Easy to Use: Tap anywhere on the screen to count. A large, clear counter shows your progress and keeps your focus on zikr.

• Statistics & Progress Tracking: Track your spiritual journey with detailed statistics showing your streaks, total counts, daily, monthly, and yearly progress. See which tasbih you use the most and monitor your consistency over time.

• Preset & Custom Tasbeehs: Start immediately with existing tasbeehs and zikr, or create your own custom tasbeeh. Save counters for daily zikr, durood, salawat, morning and evening tasbeeh, or any custom zikr you want.

• Smart Zikr Reminders: Set daily reminder notifications for your zikr and tasbeeh. The app gently alerts you to help maintain consistency in your spiritual routine.

• Auto Counter (Hands-Free Zikr): Enable the auto counter to let the tasbeeh count automatically without tapping. Choose your preferred rhythm or delay, and the app will increment the counter at the selected pace—ideal for deep focus, meditation, or continuous dhikr sessions.

• Light & Dark Mode: Switch between a clean light interface for daytime use or an eye-friendly dark interface at night.

• Vibration & Sound Feedback: Subtle vibration or sound feedback on each tap allows you to use the app without looking at the screen.

• Target & Goal Setting: Set a target for your zikr. The counter notifies you when you reach your goal, such as 33, 100, or 1000.

• Edit Your Count: Easily adjust the counter up or down if a mistake occurs.

Your All-in-One Islamic Counter App:
The app offers a complete set of features for Muslims who want a dependable digital tasbeeh.

Durood Counter: Use the dedicated durood counter or create your own to keep track of your salawat on Friday or during your daily routine.

Zikr Counter: Whether performing dhikr after salat or at any moment during the day, this zikr counter supports your spiritual practice and automatically saves your progress. You can also use the auto counter to perform zikr at a steady rhythm without manual interaction.

Islamic Tasbeeh with a Modern Touch: The app combines the familiar feel of an Islamic tasbeeh with a digital design focused on clarity and ease of use, including hands-free auto counting for uninterrupted remembrance.

Who Is This Islamic App For?
This Islamic app is designed for muslims who want to increase their remembrance of Allah. It is suitable for people replacing a physical tasbeeh, for those learning to perform zikr, and for individuals who prefer the convenience of a digital counter on their phone—whether tapping manually or using the auto counter for relaxed, continuous dhikr.

We are committed to providing a high-quality digital tasbih and zikr counter experience and continue to work on additional features and improvements based on user feedback.

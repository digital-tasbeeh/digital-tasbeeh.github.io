Welcome to Digital Tasbeeh Counter, a simple, modern, and easy-to-use tasbeeh counter app designed for your daily dhikr, salawat, dua and meditations. Are you looking for all in one zikr counter app? Need a dedicated durood counter? Or do you want to replace your physical beads with a beautiful digital tasbih app? This app is built to be a helpful companion for your daily dhikr (zikr).

You can easily count your prayers, salawat, and zikr without losing track. This Islamic tasbeeh stays in your pocket, ready whenever you need it.

Why Choose Digital Tasbeeh?
The tasbeeh counter is designed with simplicity and daily routine in mind. The focus is on offering a clean and reliable experience so you can concentrate on your prayers without distractions.

Core Features of the Best Digital Tasbeeh Counter:

• Simple & Easy to Use: Tap anywhere on the screen to count. A large, clear click counter shows your progress and keeps your focus on zikr. Perfect for beginners and experienced users alike.

• Statistics & Progress Tracking: Track your spiritual journey with detailed statistics showing your streaks, total counts, daily, monthly, and yearly progress. See which tasbih you use the most and monitor your consistency over time - ideal for Ramadan 2026 tracking.

• Preset & Custom Tasbeehs: Start immediately with existing tasbeehs and zikr, including Tasbih Fatima (33 times SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar), or create your own custom tasbeeh. Save counters for daily dhikr, durood shareef, salawat, morning and evening adhkar, or any custom wazifa you want.

• Smart Zikr Reminders: Set daily reminder notifications for your zikr and tasbeeh. The app gently alerts you to help maintain consistency in your spiritual routine and daily goals.

• Auto Counter (Hands-Free Zikr): Enable the auto counter to let the tasbeeh count automatically without tapping. Choose your preferred rhythm or delay, and the app will increment the counter at the selected pace—ideal for deep focus, meditation, or continuous dhikr sessions. This unique feature sets us apart from traditional digital tasbeeh 3D apps.

• Night Mode (OLED Black Theme): Switch between a clean light interface for daytime use or an eye-friendly dark mode optimized for night prayers like Tahajjud. The OLED black theme saves battery and prevents eye strain during late-night worship.

• Vibration & Sound Feedback: Subtle vibration feedback or sound on each tap allows you to use the app without looking at the screen - experience the real tasbeeh feel with haptic feedback.

• Target & Goal Setting: Set a target for your zikr. The counter notifies you when you reach your goal, such as 33 (Tasbih Fatima), 100, 1000, or any custom number for your daily dhikr & dua.

• Edit Your Count: Easily adjust the counter up or down if a mistake occurs. Full control over your prayer counter.

• Offline Mode: Works completely offline - no internet required. Your digital tasbeeh counter offline companion for anywhere, anytime worship.

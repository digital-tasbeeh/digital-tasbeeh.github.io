Privacy Policy for Digital Tasbeeh

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Digital Tasbeeh is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to help you with your spiritual practice while respecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Digital Tasbeeh stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Counter Data: Your dhikr counts, tasbeeh names, target counts, and progress
  • Custom Tasbeehs: Any custom tasbeehs you create with their names and settings
  • App Preferences: Your settings including sound, vibration, theme (dark/light mode), notification preferences, and prayer times
  • Statistics: Historical data about your dhikr practice for personal tracking

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Digital Tasbeeh, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Analytics: App interactions, feature usage, screen views, and session duration (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version
  • Crash Reports: Technical diagnostic information when the app crashes (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data and advertising identifiers for personalized ads (via Google AdMob)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Store and track your dhikr counts and progress
  • Manage your custom tasbeehs and preferences
  • Provide prayer time notifications
  • Display statistics and charts of your spiritual practice
  • Remember your app settings and preferences

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues and crashes
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Optimize app performance and stability

2.3 Advertising

  • Display relevant advertisements to support free app development
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Comply with advertising regulations including GDPR

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SQLite Database: For counter data, tasbeehs, and statistics
  • SharedPreferences: For app settings and preferences

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3.2 Cloud Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process data:

  • Firebase Analytics: Usage statistics and app behavior
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports and diagnostic data
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications for prayer times
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement delivery and personalization

3.3 Security Measures

  • Data is stored securely on your device using Android's security features
  • Network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We do not collect or store sensitive personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No user accounts or authentication required

4. Third-Party Services

Digital Tasbeeh integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

4.1 Google Firebase

  • Purpose: Analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications
  • Data Collected: Device information, usage patterns, crash logs
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, device information, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Ad Personalization: Manage your ad settings

5. Your Privacy Controls

You have control over certain app features and your data:

5.1 In-App Controls

  • Advertisements: Toggle ads on/off in Settings
  • Ad Frequency: Control how often ads are shown (minimum 3 minutes between ads)
  • Notifications: Enable/disable prayer time notifications
  • Sound & Vibration: Control haptic and audio feedback

5.2 Always Active Features

The following features are always enabled to ensure optimal app performance:

  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics helps us understand app usage and improve features
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics helps us identify and fix bugs quickly

5.2 Data Management

  • Reset Data: Clear all counter data and statistics from the app
  • Delete Custom Tasbeehs: Remove any custom tasbeehs you've created
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device

5.3 GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Consent: You'll be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown
  • Right to Access: Request information about data collected
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing for advertising
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Children's Privacy

Digital Tasbeeh is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app:

  • Does not require user registration or accounts
  • Does not collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Uses family-safe content and advertisements
  • Complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days
  • Advertising Data: Managed according to Google AdMob's retention policies

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States, where Firebase and Google services operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through:

  • Google's compliance with EU-US Privacy Shield and Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Firebase's security and privacy certifications
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Consent

By using Digital Tasbeeh, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. Analytics and crash reporting are essential for app functionality and improvement. You can control other features by:

  • Disabling advertisements in app settings
  • Uninstalling the application

11. No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data shared with Firebase and AdMob is solely for the purposes described in this policy (analytics, crash reporting, and advertising).

  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - California, USA
